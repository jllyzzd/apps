Product Overview

This app replaces traditional transparent plastic rulers (Li Ji Chi) used to analyze floor plans, bringing ancient wisdom to your iPhone. Overlay professional Feng Shui compasses on floor plans for precise analysis.

Key Features

1. Professional Compasses

Built-in templates for San Yuan, San He, and 24 Mountains rulers. Data is precise and rigorously verified to meet professional standards.

Switch between ruler styles using the menu in the main interface.

2. Floor Plan Overlay

Import floor plans, architectural drawings, or on-site photos. Overlay the compass on the background image to analyze Qi flow and orientation.

Tap "Select Image" to import. Use two fingers to zoom or rotate the image or compass.

3. Traditional Aesthetics & Controls

Xuan Paper texture and traditional seal-style UI. Adjust ruler transparency to keep the floor plan clearly visible underneath.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does the app need Photo Library access?

The core feature is "Floor Plan Overlay." We need access to:
1. Import floor plans or photos for analysis.
2. Save the final composite image (compass + floor plan) to your gallery.

Q: How do I rotate and resize the compass?

Rotate: Drag with one finger on the compass dial, or use a two-finger rotation gesture.
Zoom: Use a two-finger pinch gesture to scale the compass to match your floor plan.

Q: Will my data be saved if I change phones?

All data is stored locally. Deleting the app or changing devices may result in data loss. We recommend saving important results as images. Cloud backup is coming soon.

Q: What if I find an error in the compass data?

We strive for accuracy. If you notice discrepancies, please contact us via the email below and we will investigate promptly.
