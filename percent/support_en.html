About Percent

Percent is a comprehensive percentage calculation tool that supports five different types of percentage calculations, meeting your various calculation needs in daily life, study, and work.

Five Calculation Features

1. Basic Calculation

Function: Calculate what percentage of a number is

Example: What is 15% of 1000? Answer: 150

Formula: Result = Base × Percentage ÷ 100

2. Increase/Decrease

Function: Calculate the result after increasing or decreasing a number by a certain percentage

Example: What is 200 increased by 20%? Answer: 240

Example: What is 500 decreased by 10%? Answer: 450

3. Compare Calculation

Function: Calculate the change amount and change percentage from one number to another

Example: From 100 to 120, the change is +20, change percentage is +20%

4. Ratio Calculation

Function: Calculate what percentage one number is of another

Example: What percentage is 25 of 200? Answer: 12.5%

Formula: Percentage = (Part ÷ Total) × 100

5. Reverse Calculation

Function: Calculate the original value knowing the result and percentage

Example: 20% of what number is 40? Answer: 200

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I switch between calculation types?

There are five tabs at the bottom of the app, each corresponding to a different calculation type. Tap the respective tab to switch to the corresponding calculation interface.

2. How do I change the language?

Tap the language selector button (globe icon) in the top right corner to choose from 32 available languages. The app will remember your choice and automatically use your last selected language the next time you open it.

Supported languages include: Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Arabic, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Greek, Czech, Hungarian, Romanian, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Malay, Hebrew, Hindi, Urdu, Persian, Ukrainian.

3. How do I copy calculation results?

Tap the copy button next to the calculation result to copy it to the clipboard. A confirmation message will appear when the copy is successful.

4. What is the calculation precision?

The app uses Swift's Double type for calculations, ensuring high-precision results. Results are automatically formatted for display, typically showing an appropriate number of decimal places.

5. Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Percent is a completely offline app. All calculations are performed locally on your device, and all features work without an internet connection.

6. Does the app collect my data?

No. We do not collect any user data. All numbers you enter and calculation results are only stored in your device's local memory and are cleared when the app is closed. The only thing saved is your language preference, which is also stored only on your local device.

7. Why is my calculation result different from expected?

Please check the following:

  • Confirm you have selected the correct calculation type
  • Check that the numbers entered are correct
  • For the increase/decrease feature, confirm you have selected the correct operation (increase or decrease)
  • Pay attention to decimal point placement

Tips & Suggestions

  • Quick Clear: Each input field has a clear button on the right side. Tap it to quickly clear the input content
  • Auto Calculate: Results are automatically displayed after entering numbers, no need to tap a "Calculate" button
  • Smooth Switching: You can quickly switch between different calculation types, and entered data will not be lost
