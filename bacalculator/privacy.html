Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

FinCalc Pro (Bundle ID: com.linxue.BACalculator)

Privacy by design: FinCalc Pro performs financial calculations locally on your device. We do not collect personal data, do not track users, and do not upload worksheet data to remote servers.

1. Data Collection

FinCalc Pro is designed for offline use. We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number).
  • Financial worksheet inputs or calculation results.
  • Location data, contacts, photos, or microphone data.
  • Advertising identifiers, device tracking identifiers, or behavioral analytics.

2. Local Processing and Storage

All core features (TVM, Cash Flow, Bond, Date, Statistics, and Amortization) run on-device.

  • Calculation state and user preferences are stored only in app-local storage.
  • No account registration is required.
  • If you uninstall the app, local data is removed by the system.

3. Third-Party Services

FinCalc Pro does not include third-party advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or external analytics services in the current release. The App Store privacy label is aligned with Data Not Collected.

4. Children's Privacy

FinCalc Pro is a professional productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13.

5. Policy Updates

If privacy-related behavior changes in future releases, this page will be updated with a new effective date.
